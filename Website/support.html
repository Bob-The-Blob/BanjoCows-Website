FAQ

How do I Install Cheese Hunt - You download the compressed file, extract the files to wherever you want, then you double click on the Cheese Hunt.exe file. that is the file that starts the game. You can also move a shortcut to you desktop screen by right-clicking, clicking Create Shortcut, and drag the shortcut to your desktop screen.
